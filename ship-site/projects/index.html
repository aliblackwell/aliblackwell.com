2020

From social impact to projects that spark joy

  • KeyPaws

    KeyPaws is my side project that uses AI to protect your computer from cats on your keyboard.
    https://www.keypa.ws

  • Mindfulpuss

    Mindfulpuss is my podcast that encourages you to live life in the meow.
    https://www.mindfulpuss.com

  • Ask EveryDoctor

    At the height of the pandemic I developed a survey tool for campaigning organisation EveryDoctor to track PPE in the NHS.
    https://ask.everydoctor.org.uk/

  • Pembroke House

    Working with designer Rosalba Ellis again, I built a fully customisable website for Pembroke House, a centre for social action and residential community in Walworth.
    https://www.pembrokehouse.org.uk

  • JAMstack Starter

    All web projects share the same foundations. JAMstack Starter condenses my sixteen years of experience building accessible and privacy-friendly websites and apps into an open source starter template to robustly jumpstart new projects.
    https://jamstack-blaster.netlify.app/

  • Gwen Hustwit

    Made possible by JAMstack Starter, Gwen's affordable, accessible website —fully editable and customisable via a CMS—was put together in a single day of collaboration in Crystal Palace.
    https://www.gwenhustwit.com

2019

Lecturing and a return to political tech

  • Compass

    Collaborating with designer Rosalba Ellis I developed a new website for the centre-left thinktank Compass.
    https://www.compassonline.org.uk

  • Democracy Club's Election Widget

    Working in a distributed team well before it was trendy, I collaborated with the team at Democracy Club to develop a fully unit-tested, accessible polling station finder and election information widget in time for the 2019 election.
    https://democracyclub.org.uk/projects/election-widget/

  • Can I Vote?

    Another project for the 2019 election, Can I Vote highlighted to Commonwealth citizens that they are eligible to vote in UK elections.
    https://www.canivote.org.uk
